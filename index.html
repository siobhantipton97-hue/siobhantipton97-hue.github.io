Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a custom suit take in Chiang Mai?

Most custom suits are completed within 2–4 days depending on fabric and fittings.

Yes. We are located at Anusan Market on Changklan Road, steps from the Night Bazaar.

Do you offer bespoke tailoring?

Every garment is individually measured and crafted for a precise bespoke fit.

We offer wool, cashmere blends, linen, cotton and premium suiting fabrics.

Yes. We arrange fittings around travel schedules for international visitors.

Do you tailor for both men and women?

We create custom garments for both men and women.

Yes. We provide professional alterations for suits and formal garments.

Can you ship internationally?

Yes. We offer secure international shipping if required.
